Yes, i have taken #100daysofcode challenge

I was frustated with my schedule. I used to start my week with lot of plannings but always end with nothing. So, I decided to take a challenge. A challenge from myself, that will help me to follow a discipline. And ofcourse, Motivation ultimately ends with frustation. To do something or to achieve something, you have to be desciplined.

Yeah, I know that you may be not agreed with this point of view. To support my statement, lets take an example. We always start our day full of motivation. But in the end of day, we haven't achieved anything. But if we start our day full of motivation and dedication (means discipline) we will definitely achieve something in that direction.

To take this challenge, I decided to opt Instagram as my social media platform. I will daily upload my status on it and doing this will help me to document everything with proper plannings. Lets start with my documentation that what i did in these 100 Days. And after 100 days I will upload everything that i learnt in this challenge.

Week 1

  • Starting the Programming of Lab Assistant Robot.
  • Introduction to SKLearn python library and Kaggle learn.
  • Getting familiar with scikit library and machine learning.

In week 1, I was totally focused into technovation 2018. Technovation is a National prototyping contest that was organised by my college (Ajay Kumar Garg Engineering College, Ghaziabad) in assosiation with stratasys. My project was to build a Lab Assistant that will assist Lab visits, keep records of inventory available in FabLab AKGEC, will remind about meetings, Fire and smoke alarm system, authorized people in Lab, and much more.

Since, i have experince in programming embedded systems using raspberry pi. So, I started with raspberry pi python programming. Our initial task was to make a program that will convert a .txt file into audio. Using this we can achieve assisting lab visits feature. Using gTTS library, i programmed this a hour.

Now, we want to install laser pointer so that Lab Assistant can point to objects about which it is speaking. Also, motion of Robot is to be coded. But due to some college work I paused this project.

One day sitting in a lecture, i resumed my kaggle.com learning course. This day i worked on data import, display, manipulation and splitting the data into train and test sets. Then after coming home, I practised all the programs that i learnt in my lecture.

Moving to next topic, I studied Training the model using sklearn decisionTreeRegressor. In this week I became familiar with Jupyter and sklearn.
